Sage's Family!

Sage has a big family. She grew up all over the world, so traveling is one of her families favorite activities. They got to see the Eiffel Tower in Paris, France. They got to see the Leaning Tower of Pisa in Italy. They got to visit Ouarzazate, Morocco, often called the gateway to the Sahara Desert, where the family favorite movie, Gladiator, was filmed. Lastly, they got to create amazing memories with eachother.



Some of the activities they do while traveling are listed below:

  • Go into beautiful churches
  • Go on walking tours
  • Look around local markets
  • Immerse themselves in the culture
  • Eat delicious food

Sage's Family consists of 8 people. There are three older boys, and then three younger girls. They are kind of like the Brady Bunch. For Halloween one year, the Barney family dressed up as the Brady Bunch. It was a sight to see. They also love playing board games.
